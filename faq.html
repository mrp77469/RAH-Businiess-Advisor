Answers Before You Ask

Common questions about starting a business, ecommerce, Shopify, AdSense, marketing, consultations, and partnerships.

Business Startup Costs

It depends on the category. Freelancing and home-based businesses can start under PKR 100,000, while ecommerce and clothing brands typically need PKR 150,000–1,000,000. Manufacturing and import/export require significantly more. We help you find a business that fits your actual budget.

Yes. Freelancing, reselling, and certain home-based services can start with minimal capital. The tradeoff is usually a slower path to significant income, which we factor into our recommendations.

Ecommerce

No. Many sellers start on marketplaces like Daraz before investing in their own store. We usually recommend starting on a marketplace to validate demand, then building a dedicated store once you have consistent sales.

Fashion, beauty, mobile accessories, and home goods consistently perform well, but the right product depends on your budget, sourcing access, and target city. Our product research service narrows this down for your specific situation.

Shopify

Yes, especially for stores targeting international customers or wanting a professional, fast, and reliable storefront. Local payment gateway integration is available, so Pakistani customers can pay comfortably too.

A basic store can be live within one to two weeks. More complex builds with custom design, multiple product categories, or app integrations typically take three to four weeks.

Google AdSense

Approval times vary, but sites with clear niches, sufficient original content, and policy-compliant structure tend to move through review faster. Our approval guidance service focuses on getting these fundamentals right the first time.

Earnings depend heavily on niche, traffic volume, and audience location, since ad rates vary by country. We help set realistic expectations and a growth strategy based on your specific niche during consultation.

Digital Marketing

Facebook Ads generally bring faster results and suit businesses that need sales now, while SEO builds slower but more sustainable, lower-cost traffic over time. Most businesses benefit from both, sequenced based on budget.

New businesses often start with 10–20% of expected monthly revenue allocated to marketing, adjusting as you learn which channels convert. We help set a specific number during your consultation.

Consultations

Yes, the initial consultation is free and focuses on understanding your budget, goals, and experience level so we can recommend a suitable business direction.

Use the contact form on our Contact Us page, or message us directly on WhatsApp, and our team will schedule a time that works for you.

Partnerships

Investors, agencies, freelancers, and service providers who want to collaborate on business growth or ecommerce projects in Pakistan are all welcome to propose a partnership.

Our partnerships team typically responds within two business days of receiving your proposal through the Partnership page form.
